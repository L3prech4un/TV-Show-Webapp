Using Streamline
=====================

Getting Started
---------------

1. **Sign Up**: Click "Sign Up" in the navigation bar and fill out the registration form
2. **Log In**: Use your email and password to log in
3. **Explore**: Check out the home page to see what the app offers

Core Features
-------------

**Profile Management**
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

* Update your profile information
* Add shows to your "Watching", "Watched", or "Watchlist" sections
* View your posting history

**Social Features**
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

* **Create Posts**: Share your thoughts about shows you're watching
* **Comment**: Engage with other users' posts
* **Follow/Unfollow**: Connect with other TV enthusiasts
* **Discover Page**: Find new users to follow

**Media Tracking**
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

1. **Currently Watching**: Track shows you're actively watching
2. **Watched**: Log shows you've completed
3. **Watchlist**: Save shows you want to watch later

Navigation Guide
----------------

* **Home Page**: Overview of the application
* **My Feed**: Posts from users you follow
* **Discover**: Find and follow other users
* **My Profile**: Manage your watchlists and profile
* **Search Users**: Find specific users by name
* **About**: Learn more about the application

Tips for Best Experience
------------------------

* Use descriptive titles when creating posts
* Engage with other users' content through comments
* Regularly update your watchlists
* Use the search feature to find users with similar interests
